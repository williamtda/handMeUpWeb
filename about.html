About Us

Your Hand Me Downs are Someone's Hand Me Up!

  • Goal

    Our Aim is to organize motivated volunteers to deliver food that would otherwise be wasted to nearby food shelters and soup kitchens. Local communities need food, and markets need a way to deliver them. That's where you come in.

  • Now What?

    Select from a list of new deliveries on your dashboard that local markets have set up with food pantries. Google Maps directions are provided, as well as any relevant information about the two parties.

    If you run a restaurant, supermarket, or food shelter, apply to this service using the navigational links above.
